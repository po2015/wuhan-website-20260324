EOS-S520 series thermal imaging system is a high-performance monitoring platform that integrates thermal imager with daylight camera and laser range finder, and has high protection levels. It can be fixed installing on ground base, vehicle-mounted or shipborne. It can be a stand-alone system or can be integrated into existing net-
works.
Configuration and Features
Thermal Camera
Thermal camera is equipped with cooled 640x512 MCT detector, it gives you clear crisp image with embedded ad- vance image processing technology. Combined with a 110-1100mm or 35-700mm continuous zoom lens, it is suitable for use in dark, complex weather conditions and provides clear thermal image for distant targets.
Daylight Camera
Daylight camera is equipped with 1/1.8” CMOS detector which provides more crisp detail and colorful image than that of 1/3”, it will also have more powerful ability to see target through fog and haze. A 60X optical zoom lens, up to focus 1200mm, is for extreme long range detection.
Pan-Tilt
S520 adopts high precision direct drive motor, which makes the equipment rotate more smoothly, position more ac- curate, response more timely. The positioning accuracy is up to 0.003°.
Image tracking function
Video tracking is based on a powerful video processor with powerful video processing and analysis functions and AI learning capabilities. It can process the video signal, overlay the system status character, and output to the monitor and video recorder through the form of network video stream. Through AI deep learning algorithm, it can realize the function of automatic identification and tracking of various targets. In tracking mode, the platform can automatically turn as the tracked target moves, and the user can select the target to track in the thermal or visible image.
Laser Range Finder
An eye safe Laser Range Finder is equipped in the system to detect the distance of the target up to 20Km.
