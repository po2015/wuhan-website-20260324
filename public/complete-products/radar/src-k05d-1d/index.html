Radar Product

SRC-K05D-1D Radar

SRC-K05D-1D is a Ku band Pulse Doppler radar for drone detection, using 2D electronic scanning + mechanical rotation and supporting civil security surveillance out to 5 km.

Ku band 2D electronic scanning + mechanical rotation Pulse Doppler Max 5 km Factory Xi'an, China

Overview

Product Positioning

SRC-K05D-1D is positioned for drone detection missions that require reliable target detection, practical deployment, and stable all-weather operation in civil security projects.

Typical use cases include Low-altitude security for borders, industrial sites, and energy facilities, Counter-UAS early warning for ports, airports, and coastal infrastructure, Rapid deployment radar support for temporary security operations, while the source material indicates a practical operating range of up to 5 km for the main surveillance task profile. The platform uses Mechanical scanning And Two-Dim Phased Scanning to support continuous monitoring and target tracking.

SRC-K05D-1D can be integrated into layered civil security systems that combine radar, EO/IR verification, communications, and command software. For projects focused on drone detection, the model is suitable for deployments such as Low-altitude security for borders, industrial sites, and energy facilities, Counter-UAS early warning for ports, airports, and coastal infrastructure, Rapid deployment radar support for temporary security operations.

FAQ

Who manufactures SRC-K05D-1D, and what is Cyrentis's role in the project?

Cyrentis acts as a solution provider and international distributor rather than the original radar manufacturer. The SRC-K05D-1D hardware is produced by specialized radar manufacturers in China, while Cyrentis focuses on model selection, technical coordination, integration consulting, export process support, and global project delivery.

Can SRC-K05D-1D integrate with EO/IR systems, command platforms, or countermeasure subsystems?

Yes. SRC-K05D-1D can be integrated with EO/IR tracking payloads, command-and-control platforms, communication networks, and other civil security subsystems through standard interfaces and open integration workflows. Cyrentis can also support complete integrated solutions that combine radar, optics, communications, and software.

Is SRC-K05D-1D suitable for rapid deployment or mobile use?

Yes. SRC-K05D-1D is intended for practical field deployment with standard mounting arrangements and clear engineering interfaces. With trained teams, the system can be installed, commissioned, and redeployed efficiently for both fixed and mobile security tasks.

How does SRC-K05D-1D handle false alarms and complex environments?

The source material emphasizes low false-alarm behavior, stable tracking, and operation in demanding outdoor environments. In integrated deployments, the radar can also work together with higher-level classification workflows and visual verification tools to improve practical target discrimination.

Is SRC-K05D-1D available for unrestricted export?

No. SRC-K05D-1D is intended for civil security applications only, and radar systems are subject to dual-use export control regulations of the People's Republic of China. International delivery requires the appropriate licensing procedures before shipment.
