It provides all-day, all-weather continuous detection and tracking of moving targets within the area, featuring high detection sensitivity, low false alarm rate, and high precision.
Product Advantages：High data rate and strong tracking capability; low probability of false alarms; wide detection range with high accuracy; cost-effective and easy to operate.
Application Scenarios：Border Surveillance, Maritime Surveillance, Key Site Protection, Battlefield Reconnaissance.
