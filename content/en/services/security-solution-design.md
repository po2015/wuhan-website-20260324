This service focuses on turning site requirements into a workable security solution. Cyrentis helps define how Horizon, sensor families, and field subsystems should be combined to meet coverage, verification, and operational goals.

Typical deliverables include system architecture, sensor-role planning, deployment logic, workflow mapping, and integration recommendations for the intended monitoring scenario.
