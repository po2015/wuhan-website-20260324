Cyrentis Horizon acts as the central software layer of a multi-sensor monitoring system. It is designed to receive, organize, and present sensing data from radar, electro-optical, and RF devices in one operational environment.

Horizon is typically deployed together with field sensors rather than as a standalone software package. The platform supports:

- device connectivity across multiple sensing technologies
- fused monitoring and target correlation
- visualization of sites, tracks, and alarm events
- integration with external command or management platforms

This structure allows operators to move from detection to verification and response without switching between isolated subsystems.
