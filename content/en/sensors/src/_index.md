Surveillance Radar is the primary detection layer of a Cyrentis monitoring system. The `SRC` family is designed for early warning and wide-area surveillance across different environments.

Typical detection targets include:

- small UAVs and fixed-wing UAVs
- human movement
- vehicles
- vessels

SRC models can differ in waveform type, frequency band, detection-range class, and scanning architecture. They are often paired with Surveillance Optics and managed through the Horizon platform.
