Spectrum Detection focuses on RF spectrum awareness. The `SDC` family monitors wireless activity to help identify emitters, detect suspicious control links, and improve awareness of the low-altitude electromagnetic environment.

Typical capabilities include:

- RF signal detection
- wireless emitter monitoring
- UAV control-link awareness
- integration with multi-sensor alert workflows

Depending on the project, SDC systems can operate independently or be integrated with Horizon together with radar and electro-optical devices.
