## Product Positioning

The SRC-X05B-2D moving target surveillance radar adopts azimuth mechanical scanning and elevation digital beamforming technology. It provides all-day, all-weather continuous detection and tracking of moving targets within the designated area, offering advantages such as high detection sensitivity, low false alarm rate, and high precision.

Typical use cases include Border surveillance, Maritime surveillance, Key site protection, while the source material indicates a practical operating range of up to 10 km for the main surveillance task profile. The platform uses Azimuth Mechanical Scanning, Elevation Digital Beamforming (DBF) System to support continuous monitoring and target tracking.
