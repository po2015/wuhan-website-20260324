## Product Positioning

The SRC-K02E-2G moving target surveillance radar is specifically designed for the detection and real-time tracking of moving targets on the ground and water surfaces. It serves as an all-weather, day-and-night efficient surveillance system, capable of stable operation even in complex environments and harsh weather conditions. Employing a Digital Beamforming (DBF) system, the radar can accurately detect and track various types of targets, providing detailed information such as distance, speed, azimuth, and type. It features functions including boundary crossing alerts, early warnings, and recording capabilities.

Typical use cases include Land border surveillance, Maritime surveillance, Critical site protection, while the source material indicates a practical operating range of up to 5 km for the main surveillance task profile. The platform uses Integrated DBF technology and multi-phase center angle measurement technology. to support continuous monitoring and target tracking.
