## Product Positioning

The SRC-X05E-3D moving target surveillance radar employs a two-dimensional electronic scanning system, enabling continuous, all-day, all-weather detection and tracking of moving targets within its coverage area. It offers advantages such as high detection sensitivity, low false alarm rate, and excellent accuracy.

Typical use cases include Border surveillance, Maritime surveillance, Critical site protection, while the source material indicates a practical operating range of up to 8 km for the main surveillance task profile. The platform uses Two-Dimensional Electronic Scanning System to support continuous monitoring and target tracking.
