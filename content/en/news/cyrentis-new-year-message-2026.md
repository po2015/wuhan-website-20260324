Cyrentis shared its 2026 New Year message with thanks to customers, project partners, suppliers, and collaborators who supported the company during its first stage of development.

In the message, the company reaffirmed three priorities for the new year. The first is to continue clarifying the Horizon software direction so the platform can be presented as a coherent operational system rather than a collection of isolated functions. The second is to strengthen the `SRC`, `SOC`, and `SDC` product families so visitors can understand where radar, electro-optics, and RF awareness each fit in a layered monitoring architecture. The third is to improve the company’s project-facing delivery capability through system design, software customization, and equipment integration support.

Cyrentis also stated that 2026 will be a year of practical refinement. Instead of trying to present too many disconnected claims, the company will continue improving the clarity of its public product structure, deployment logic, and customer-facing system story.

The company wishes all customers, partners, and friends a healthy, productive, and successful new year, and looks forward to building clearer monitoring systems together in 2026.
