Wuhan Cyrentis Technology introduced its operating structure around three connected areas: the Horizon software platform, sensor products, and service capabilities covering software customization, solution design, and equipment integration.

The company stated that future delivery will focus on multi-sensor monitoring workflows that combine radar, electro-optical observation, and RF awareness through one operational software layer.
