Cyrentis announced in December 2025 that it had signed a new customer contract for electro-optical equipment supply and project support, marking another step in moving the `SOC` product family from product definition into actual delivery activity.

The contract scope centers on electro-optical monitoring equipment, configuration alignment, and project-side coordination related to deployment planning. Cyrentis stated that the work will include not only hardware delivery, but also support around equipment fit, control-room workflow expectations, and interface planning for the wider monitoring system.

For Cyrentis, the contract is important because it validates the company's approach of presenting electro-optical products as part of a broader system architecture rather than as isolated camera assets. The company expects the `SOC` family to support projects that need visual confirmation, target follow-up, and coordinated use together with radar, platform software, or other site systems.

The business team also noted that this kind of contract supports the same direction shown across the wider Cyrentis offering: software through Horizon, product supply through the sensor families, and deployment value through project design and integration support.
